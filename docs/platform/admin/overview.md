Administration covers the settings that apply to the whole organisation — who can sign in, which AI providers are available, how the product looks, what rules govern content, and how usage is tracked. These pages are for the **Admin** and **Owner** roles; other roles cannot see the admin surface.

Start with [members and roles](/platform/admin/members-and-roles) — the canonical permission matrix is the reference every other role page in this tree links back to. Then configure [authentication](/self-hosted/admin/authentication) (password, SSO, or trusted headers), connect [AI providers](/platform/admin/providers), and apply [branding](/platform/admin/branding) to match your organisation.

## In this section

- [Members and roles](/platform/admin/members-and-roles) — six-role permission model.
- [Teams](/platform/admin/teams) — scope knowledge and chat access.
- [Authentication](/self-hosted/admin/authentication) — password, SSO, trusted headers.
- [Providers](/platform/admin/providers) — LLM and email provider configuration.
- [Branding](/platform/admin/branding) — logos, colours, and product name.
- [Governance](/platform/admin/governance) — content and policy controls.
- [Usage analytics](/platform/admin/usage-analytics) — per-user and org-wide activity.
