## AI Chat Assistant

Chat with an AI assistant that understands your business context and can take actions on your behalf.

### What You Can Do

| Capability | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Natural Conversation** | Ask questions in plain language; the AI remembers your entire conversation |
| **Upload Files** | Attach images, PDFs, or documents for the AI to analyze |
| **Get Real-time Responses** | See the AI's thinking process as it works on your request |
| **Search Your Data** | Ask about customers, products, orders, or any business data |
| **Generate Documents** | Create PDFs, Excel files, presentations, and more through chat |
| **Automate Tasks** | Describe what you want automated, and the AI builds workflows for you |

### Examples

- "Show me all customers who haven't purchased in 90 days"
- "Generate a PDF report of this month's sales"
- "What's our return policy?" (searches your knowledge base)
- "Create a workflow that sends welcome emails to new customers"

## Document Generation

Create professional documents directly from the platform.

### Supported Formats

| Format | What You Can Do |
|--------|-----------------|
| **PDF** | Generate reports, invoices, or documents from text, HTML, or web pages |
| **Excel** | Export data to spreadsheets with multiple sheets and custom formatting |
| **PowerPoint** | Create presentations from templates with your branding |
| **Word** | Generate documents from templates with structured content |
| **Images** | Capture screenshots from web pages or generate images from content |

### Document Parsing

Upload existing documents to extract their content:

- **PDF**: Extract text from uploaded PDF files
- **PowerPoint**: Analyze slide structure and content
- **Word**: Extract text and structure from documents

### Examples

- "Create an Excel file with all active products"
- "Generate a PDF report of churned customers"
- "Take a screenshot of our homepage"
- "Extract text from this PDF" (with file upload)

## Knowledge Base

Build a searchable knowledge base that the AI can reference when answering questions.

### How It Works

1. **Upload Documents**: PDFs, Word files, and other documents are automatically indexed
2. **Add Information**: Tell the AI to remember policies, procedures, or FAQs
3. **Search Semantically**: Ask questions in natural language, not just keywords
4. **Keep Updated**: Correct outdated information through conversation

### What Gets Indexed

| Source | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Uploaded Documents** | PDFs, Word files, and other documents you upload |
| **Website Content** | Pages from your website (via website scanning) |
| **Manual Entries** | Information you tell the AI to remember |
| **Product Catalog** | Your product information for recommendations |
| **Customer Data** | Customer information for personalized responses |

### Examples

- "What is our shipping policy?"
- "Remember: Our store hours are 9am-5pm on weekdays"
- "That's incorrect. Update the return policy to 30 days"
- "Find information about sustainability in our products"

## Workflow Automation

Automate repetitive tasks and complex business processes.

### How to Create Workflows

1. **Describe in Chat**: Tell the AI what you want to automate in plain language
2. **Visual Editor**: Use the drag-and-drop workflow builder
3. **Templates**: Start from predefined workflow templates

### What You Can Automate

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Customer Engagement** | Welcome emails, re-engagement campaigns, birthday messages |
| **Data Sync** | Import products from Shopify, sync customers from external systems |
| **AI-Powered Responses** | Auto-reply to customer inquiries with personalized messages |
| **Notifications** | Alert team members about important events |
| **Content Processing** | Scan websites, index documents, update knowledge base |
| **Approvals** | Route sensitive actions for human review |

### Workflow Triggers

| Trigger | When It Runs |
|---------|--------------|
| **Scheduled** | Run at specific times (hourly, daily, weekly) |
| **Manual** | Run on demand when you trigger it |
| **Event-Based** | Run when specific events occur (new customer, new order) |

### Examples

- "Create a workflow that sends welcome emails to new customers"
- "Build an automation that identifies at-risk customers and sends them offers"
- "Show me all my active workflows"
- "Create a daily report of new orders"
